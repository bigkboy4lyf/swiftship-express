Freight Transportation Options

Less Than Truckload (LTL)

Cost-effective for shipments that don't require a full truck. Perfect for 1-6 pallets.

Full Truckload (FTL)

Dedicated truck for your shipment only. Best for large volume or time-sensitive freight.

Ocean Freight

Economical solution for international shipments. Container (FCL) or shared (LCL) options.

Air Freight

Fastest international option for time-sensitive cargo. Door-to-door service available.

Freight Service Comparison

Service Type Best For Transit Time Cost Efficiency

Freight Class

Freight class (NMFC) determines pricing. Factors include density, value, handling, and liability. We help determine the correct class for your shipment.
