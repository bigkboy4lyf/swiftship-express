International Shipping Process

Step 1: Documentation & Preparation

Our experts help prepare all necessary shipping documents, including commercial invoices, certificates of origin, and customs declarations.

Step 2: Customs Clearance

We handle all customs procedures, duty calculations, and tax payments on your behalf, ensuring smooth border crossings.

Step 3: International Transit

Your shipment travels via air or sea freight, depending on urgency and cost requirements, with real-time tracking throughout.

Step 4: Local Delivery

Our local partners in the destination country handle the final delivery, ensuring compliance with local regulations and customs.
